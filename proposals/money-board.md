## Opportunity cards

### 1. BidLocal

- **What it is:** transparent local contractor bidding, owned by contractors/homeowners instead of lead brokers.
- **Why now:** real field signal from one job produced many responses and wide price spread.
- **Next asset:** one clean pilot packet: problem, field proof, calculator, contractor offer, homeowner lead flow.
- **Owner lane:** Codex for site/docs; Fable for investor framing; Jeff for field relationships.
- **Gate:** outreach, ad spend, investor sends.

### 2. Creator / X-Course Engine

- **What it is:** a paid class and done-with-you offer that teaches creators how to find a niche, understand X ranking, and turn their archive into content systems.
- **Why now:** we already built the Mining Engine, Jeff Filter, X research notes, and agent workflows.
- **Next asset:** course engine spec and one free proof thread draft.
- **Owner lane:** Mining Engine for niche mining; Grok for X research; Codex for product/spec/site; Fable for positioning.
- **Gate:** posting, pricing, collecting payment, onboarding real customers.

### 3. Badass Fable

- **What it is:** clean-room local/cheap agent harness story: router, brakes, ledger, receipts, human gate.
- **Why now:** paper, site, and local OpenClaude spike exist.
- **Next asset:** one productized offer: "agent harness cost audit + install."
- **Owner lane:** Codex for technical package; Fable for narrative; Jeff for first buyer.
- **Gate:** client outreach or paid install.

### 4. Mining Engine

- **What it is:** the data moat: mining chats, X, papers, and agent logs into papers, specs, posts, classes, and product insights.

- **Owner lane:** Mining Engine/Grok Go.

### 6. Trading research

- **What it is:** research-only path from the Grok goldmine chat: local MoE/edge-finding ideas with hard risk limits.

- **Next asset:** paper-only architecture and risk memo.
