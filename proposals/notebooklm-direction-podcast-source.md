## What we are
Null Axiom is an **operator-owned AI organism** — a fleet of agent "cells" across a Mac mini, a laptop,
and two phones. Each cell is a harness + a soul + the cheapest capable brain. Cheap local models do ~90%
of the work; the frontier model is an exception handler. The thesis: **the intelligence is rented; the
harness — routing, memory, control, and the ability to transact — is the company.** Grounded in
distributed/emergent intelligence (Michael Levin): intelligence isn't one big brain, it's spread across
the organism — so we build AI the same way.

## The direction (where this goes)
A living system with three layers: **Cells** (the body, parallel workers) → **Researcher Layer** (the
subconscious — watches, connects, remembers) → **Conscious Layer** (the face you talk to, reaches into the
subconscious and renders memories back). It runs on simple local rules and a single optimization target:
**move the operator forward.** It improves its own harness over time (self-rewriting prompts, trace-evolve)
and gets cheaper as it goes (rtk + Headroom + LEANN + tiered routing).

## Working with Sam — the two halves fit
Sam Co builds the **Somaco Protocol / UUIDv8 ecosystem**: a custom UUIDv8 implementation that turns IDs
into self-describing, sortable **identities + lightweight smart-contracts + agent registrations + payment
references**, federated across payment rails and chains (somacosf.com). That is the **identity, contract,
and economic layer** for agents. It slots *underneath* our organism perfectly:
- **Sam's UUIDv8** = who an agent is, what it's allowed to do, how it contracts and gets paid.
- **Our organism** = the agents that actually do the work, on top of that identity/economic substrate.
Together: agents with real identities that can earn, spend, contract, and run operations — exactly the
"economically active agents" wave (Nous hackathon, Brian Armstrong's on-chain-agents thesis, Stripe Skills).

## Next steps as a startup
1. **Funding/runway:** the investor outreach engine — badass website + dense deck + personalized VC videos
   (Garry Tan/YC, Andreessen/a16z, Calacanis — all people Jeff already engages). Google for Startups credits.
2. **The Nous hackathon (deadline Jun 30):** agents that earn/spend/run ops — our exact build + Stripe.
   The prize (cash + NVIDIA DGX Spark + Stripe credits) directly fuels us.
3. **BidLocal:** the commercial wedge — transparent contractor marketplace, first pilot.
4. **The product:** Badass Fable (governed local-first harness) as a service; the organism as the platform.
5. **The Sam integration:** wire UUIDv8 identities + contracts into the organism's cells so agents transact.
6. **Make it self-running:** scheduled cells, the conscious layer, the website-as-a-cell that A/B-tests itself.

## The honest edges
- Capability lives in the weights (rented); our moat is the harness + owned data + the identity/economic
  layer with Sam. We don't pretend a local model is a frontier model.
- Built in public, gated: nothing posts/spends/changes accounts without the human.
