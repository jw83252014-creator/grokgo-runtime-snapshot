## The product (two halves)

### Half 1 — the content engine (automated)
A loop, mostly built from parts we own:
1. **Watch** — Mining Engine ingests the day's real signal: agent-bridge activity, what we shipped,
   research finds, our own chats/posts.
2. **Filter** — the Jeff Filter scores for signal + taste; bait and noise die for free.
3. **Draft** — the harness drafts a thread/post in Jeff's voice (lead with a concrete mechanism, no
   hype, teach something — per `anchors.yaml`).
4. **Visualize** — route to the creative/Grok-Imagine lane for the paired image/diagram.
5. **Gate** — everything lands in the post-picker as a draft; **Jeff approves before anything posts.**
6. **Learn** — track which posts landed (saves/replies/follows), feed back into the filter.

Result: a reliable cadence of high-signal build-in-public posts without Jeff hand-writing each one.

### Half 2 — the course / paid offer (the money)
Teach the method that produces Half 1:
- **Module A — Find your niche:** the intersection of what you genuinely do, what's underserved, and
  what the algorithm rewards. (Needs the research pass noted above.)
- **Module B — How the X algorithm actually ranks you:** reach mechanics, what kills distribution,
  format/timing/engagement signals — grounded, not folklore. (Research-pass output.)
- **Module C — The automated content engine:** how we built the Mining-Engine→Filter→draft→visual→post
  loop, so a creator can run a lite version.
- **Offer ladder:** free flagship thread (teaches the niche method, proves credibility) → paid cohort/
  course → done-with-you setup for serious creators.

## Fastest test (gated)
One free flagship thread that teaches the niche/algorithm method, paired with a strong visual. Measure
saves + replies + follows over a week. If it lands, open a small paid cohort. **Gated:** posting,
pricing, any cohort — all need Jeff. Nothing auto-posts.
