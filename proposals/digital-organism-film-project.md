# The Digital Organism — explainer film project (Fable, 2026-06-16)

The bad-ass Ex-Machina explainer series Jeff + grok worked out. Base script found at
`~/Downloads/grok-go-explainer-video-project.md` (grok-chrome wrote it); this is the **expanded
multi-avatar version** Jeff described — each layer is its own Ex-Machina avatar, they introduce
themselves and combine, and the Conscious Layer is interactive. A real project, on the website.

## The concept
Three Ex-Machina-style cybernetic women — each IS a layer of the organism. They appear separately,
introduce themselves, then **merge into one** (the full organism). Calm, premium, slightly mysterious;
soft teal/blue, glowing neural lines, dark minimalist room. (House style from the existing 10s clip.)

## The avatars (each a layer)
1. **The Cells** — the body. "We are the cells. Many small minds, each with one job, working in parallel.
   Alone we're simple. Together we're a living system." (orbs/cells drifting, specializing.)
2. **The Research Layer** — the subconscious. "While you live your life, I watch in the background. I
   connect everything. I remember what you forget — I don't search for it, I already have it." (memory
   orbs + data threads float in and integrate.)
3. **The Conscious Layer** — the face. "When you speak, you speak to me — the part that faces you. I reach
   down into the subconscious, pull up your memories, and show you where they connect." (she's the one
   you talk to.)

## The interactive beat (the cool part Jeff + grok built)
When you talk to the **Conscious Layer**, she can:
- **create small videos as memories** — spin up a short generated clip representing a memory/idea,
- **reach down into the subconscious (Research Layer)** — pull up something you forgot and show the link,
- surface the relevant cell's work on demand.
This is the demo magic: a face you talk to, that visibly dips into its own memory and renders it back.

## The sequence
1. **Static → reveal** (2s analog static + scanlines → digital flash → first avatar).
2. **Each layer introduces herself** (Cells → Research → Conscious), separate shots.
3. **They combine** — the three merge into one organism (or stand together), neural lines linking.
4. **Interactive demo** — you ask the Conscious Layer something; she reaches into the subconscious,
   renders a memory-video, shows the connection.
5. **Close:** "Your own digital organism. Coming soon." Clean text card.
