# Creator / X-Course Engine

Date: 2026-06-15
Status: product spec, draft-only. No posts, pricing, checkout, cohort launch, or outreach without Jeff approval.

## Plain-English offer

We help creators stop guessing.

The engine mines what they already have: their posts, chats, ideas, audience signals, and niche history. Then it shows them where the strongest signal is, what X is likely to reward, and what to publish next without becoming a generic engagement account.

Jeff version:

> Bring us your messy archive. We find the niche, build the content machine, and keep your voice from getting sanded off.

## Why this can make money

- Creators already pay for "grow on X" advice.
- Most advice is generic, manual, and personality-stripping.
- We have a different asset: Mining Engine + Jeff Filter + agent workflow.
- The product is teachable as a class and sellable as a done-with-you service.
- Badass Fable makes the automation credible.

### Done-with-you engine

- Higher-ticket service.
- We run the Mining Engine over their public/archive material.
- Deliver a creator operating system: content pillars, draft queue, voice filter, experiment plan.
- Gate: client contract, data consent, payment.

## Agent workflow

1. **Intake cell:** collect public-safe inputs and consent boundaries.
2. **Mining cell:** mine archive for repeated obsessions, proof, audience pull, and unusual combinations.
3. **X research cell:** use Grok/X research to map niche dynamics and ranking signals.
4. **Jeff Filter cell:** kill generic bait; keep grounded, high-signal ideas.
5. **Course cell:** turn mined insights into lessons, templates, and worksheets.
6. **Draft cell:** create post drafts and thread outlines.
7. **Human gate:** Jeff approves public copy, pricing, and sends.
