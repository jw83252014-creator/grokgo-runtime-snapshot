# X growth playbook (Jeff's strategy, 2026-06-19)

## The strategy (works — Jeff's proven moves)
1. **Amplify winners.** When a post pops (fast views/replies), extend it: self-reply/quote-tweet to add
   depth and re-ride the algorithm (replies/QTs are weighted far above likes).
2. **Comment-drop on big AI posts.** Drop a relevant, value-adding comment (with your link/angle) under
   high-traffic AI accounts — Mario Nawafal, a16z / Marc Andreessen, etc. — to borrow their reach.
3. **Follow-for-follow in comment sections.** On a big AI post, follow active commenters; many follow
   back. Compounds your network in the AI space.

## The trigger
A post is "popping" when it clears the early-velocity window (the playbook: first 30–90 min decide reach).
Practical trigger: ~50+ views or 2+ replies in the first 30 min → amplify immediately.

## ⚠️ THE LINE (this is the one that got us the "platform manipulation" label)
**X enforces hard against AUTOMATED posting, commenting, following, and liking on a personal account.**
Bots that auto-comment/auto-follow = exactly what triggered the label. We do NOT cross this again.

So the split is:
- ✅ **SAFE (assist, human acts):** an "amplify assistant" that *detects* a popping post and *drafts*
  everything for you to tap — the self-reply text, 2–3 tailored comments for specific big posts (with the
  target post links), and a list of commenters worth following. All delivered to your Telegram. **You tap.**
- ⛔ **NOT SAFE (don't build):** bots that auto-reply, auto-comment under others, or auto-follow on your
  personal account. That's the manipulation line. One-tap-by-you keeps it legitimate engagement.

## The build (safe version)
1. **Watcher:** poll your own post's stats (via the logged-in browser, read-only). When it crosses the
   trigger, fire.
2. **Drafter (Fable):** generate (a) a self-reply/QT to extend, (b) comments tailored to 2–3 current big
   AI posts I find, (c) a shortlist of commenters to follow.
3. **Deliver to Telegram** as a tap-list: "Reply this → [text]", "Comment under [link] → [text]",
   "Follow these 8 → [handles]". You execute in ~60 seconds.
4. Optionally: keep a running list of AI accounts you've matched with (follow-back network) so we target
   the warmest ones.

## Why assist-not-automate is actually better
- No enforcement risk (your account stays clean).
- Your comments read as genuinely yours (specificity + a real face = the credibility bots can't fake).
- You still get the speed: the draft is ready in seconds; you just tap.
