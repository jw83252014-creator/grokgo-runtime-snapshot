## Tone

Clean explainer. Curious but truth-seeking. Do not present speculative claims as established science.

## Script

Some people describe intuition as a kind of signal problem.

In this framework, the "dragon" is not just a fantasy creature. It is a symbol for compressed memory, emotional pattern, and deep self-knowledge across time.

The practical claim is simple enough: if your inner state is fragmented, the signal gets noisy. If you process the emotional charge, the signal gets cleaner.

That part maps surprisingly well to agent systems. Bad memory, unresolved state, and noisy context make bad outputs. Coherence matters.

Where we have to be careful is the mechanism. Claims about DNA tuning, past-life memory, and non-local perception are speculative. Interesting, but not established science.

So the useful move is to separate the layers:

one, the symbolic protocol;
two, the psychological practice;
three, the scientific evidence;
four, the open hypothesis.

That is how a research cell should treat this kind of material: preserve the signal, label the evidence, and make it understandable without pretending certainty we do not have.
