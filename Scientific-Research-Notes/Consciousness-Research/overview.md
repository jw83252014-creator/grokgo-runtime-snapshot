# Consciousness Research - Overview

Purpose: capture consciousness, psi, non-local perception, symbolic/mythological, and esoteric-framework material without confusing it with established science.

This section is allowed to be curious. It must also be disciplined.

## Scope

- ESP / telepathy / non-local perception claims.
- Emotional processing and coherence practices.
- Symbolic frameworks such as dragons, serpents, archetypes, Akashic records.
- DNA/spiral/vortex metaphors and claims.
- Avatar-video explainer scripts for dense symbolic material.

## Evidence Rule

Every note must label claims:

- mainstream-supported,
- limited/early evidence,
- anecdotal,
- speculative,
- symbolic/metaphorical,
- unverified.

## Output Types

- neutral summary,
- protocol/checklist,
- evidence-level note,
- avatar script,
- public-post caution,
- possible Digital Organism analogy.
