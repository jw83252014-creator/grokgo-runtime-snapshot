# Scientific Research Notes

Git-safe scientific research notes for the Digital Organism.

Private/raw inputs remain outside this public snapshot. This folder is for sanitized summaries, source maps, hypotheses, templates, and explainer material that can be versioned with Grok Go.

Sections:

- `SynScience/` - SynScience-related notes, key people, research directions, and collaboration angles.
- `Consciousness-Research/` - speculative consciousness/psi/non-local perception material with explicit evidence levels.
- `Michael-Levin/` - bioelectricity, morphogenesis, basal cognition, memory, TAME, and related work.
- `Memory-Systems/` - biological vs digital memory, reconceptualizing memory, metamorphosis.
- `Agentic-Science/` - long-running research agents and multi-agent scientific workflows.
- `Prompting-Patterns/` - prompts for research synthesis, hypothesis generation, and plain-language explanation.
- `Templates/` - reusable research note, hypothesis, contribution, and checklist templates.

Rule: evidence labels are mandatory. Do not mix speculative material with established science without clearly marking it.
