# SynScience - Research Directions

Initial directions inferred from Jeff-provided post analyses:

- persistent research agents for scientific discovery,
- synthetic biology + AI agent workflows,
- structured knowledge bases for scientific memory,
- multi-agent workflows for literature synthesis, modeling, explanation, and hypothesis generation,
- accessible public explainers for advanced biological computation and memory topics.

## Grok Go Fit

The strongest fit is not "we are the biology experts." It is tooling:

- persistent Researcher Layer,
- Tool Cells for source capture and synthesis,
- receipts and evidence labels,
- Fable for hard framing,
- Jade/local models for cheap scanning,
- Git knowledge base for compounding context.
